About Institute

SAPDJ Pathashala – A legacy of value-based education since 1885.

SAPDJ Pathashala, Solapur

The doyen of the Jain Community, Shri Seth Hirachand Nemchand, in his assiduous endeavor to serve society, established Shri Aillak Pannalal Digambar Jain Pathashala (S.A.P.D.J.P.) in the year 1885.

Originally started with the objective of imparting religious education, the trust gradually expanded into the field of general education and established high schools. Since its establishment, the institution has continuously worked in the interest of society.

SAPDJ Pathashala is one of the rare institutions with a glorious history of more than 125 years. The Pathashala witnessed its growth under the guidance of the pioneer of Indian industries and an epitome of service to society — Shriman Sheth Walchand and Sheth Lalchand.

The institution follows the Panchasutri of Knowledge, Character, Love, Service, and Management to strengthen liberty, equality, and brotherhood in society.

In its 75th year, the trust established Solapur College of Arts, Science and Commerce. Since then, the trust has expanded with the dual objective of promoting Shramana Culture and protecting educational culture.

Vision

To develop competent students through value-based quality education, committed to ethical and multi-dimensional development for social and financial well-being.

Mission

To cater to academic and professional aspirations through value-based education, promoting marginalized students and supplying ethical, globally competent entrepreneurs, managers, and businessmen.

Our Goals

  • To cater to academic and professional aspirations of students.
  • To supply ethical human capital in the form of entrepreneurs, innovators, managers, and businessmen.
  • To develop students using head, hand, and heart in a balanced manner.
  • To promote eco-conscious professional attitudes for sustainable development.
  • To encourage social sensitization through value-based education.
  • To support marginalized students and uphold social equality.
  • To equip entrepreneurs to contribute meaningfully to society.
