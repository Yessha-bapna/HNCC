MBA (Full-Time Program)

A comprehensive two-year postgraduate program designed to build strong foundations in management, leadership, and strategic thinking.

Program Overview

The MBA (Full-Time) program at HNCC MBA is a rigorous and immersive management program aimed at developing competent professionals equipped with analytical skills, ethical values, and leadership capabilities.

The curriculum integrates core management principles with practical exposure, case-based learning, and industry interaction to ensure holistic development of students.

This program is ideal for fresh graduates and early-career professionals seeking structured learning and long-term career growth in business and management.

Program Snapshot

  • Full-Time, Residential Program
  • Duration: Two Academic Years
  • Choice-Based Credit System
  • Industry-oriented curriculum
  • Strong focus on ethics and leadership

Key Program Highlights

A curriculum designed to balance academic rigor with practical exposure.

Strong Academic Foundation

Comprehensive coverage of core management areas including Finance, Marketing, Human Resources, Operations, and Strategy.

Industry Exposure

Learning through case studies, industry interactions, guest lectures, and practical projects.

Leadership Development

Focus on communication skills, teamwork, ethical decision-making, and professional leadership capabilities.

Eligibility

Candidates must have completed a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university. Admissions are subject to institutional norms, entrance examinations, and merit-based selection.

Program Duration

The MBA (Full-Time) program spans two academic years, divided into semesters and conducted as per university and regulatory guidelines.
