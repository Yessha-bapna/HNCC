Eligibility Criteria

Basic academic requirements for admission to MBA programs.

Academic Qualification

Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university with the minimum qualifying percentage prescribed by the institution.

Final year students awaiting results may also apply, subject to submission of proof of qualification at the time of admission.

Eligibility Snapshot

  • Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university
  • Minimum qualifying marks as per norms
  • Final year students may apply
  • Subject to university regulations

Additional Requirements

Supporting criteria considered during the admission process.

Entrance Tests

Scores from recognized entrance examinations may be considered as per institutional and regulatory guidelines.

Personal Interview

Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview to assess aptitude, communication, and career orientation.

Merit-Based Selection

Final selection is based on academic performance, entrance scores, and overall merit.
